s3-blogger
Automated S3 static blogs

I wanted to build a simple solution for hosting a blog - no servers, no frameworks, no money needed.

Simply clone this template repo, add your AWS keys and write markdown files, and every time you push to the main branch, a CircleCI pipeline will turn your markdown into HTML files, transpile your SASS code and upload everything to your S3 bucket of choice.

Technologies:

Node SASS CircleCI AWS S3

Levski Fight Club
An online schedule page for my fight gym

The only way you could check the schedule for our fight gym was by going on their Facebook page and looking at a pinned photo, uploaded by the admin.

I decided to build a page that will display the schedule and allow our coaches to update it instantly. Since none of them wanted to deal with logging into a CMS, pushing buttons (why do that, when you could spend that time training?), I utilised a Google Sheet as a database for the coaches to update. The front end is a Vue.js app that calls a NestJS API, fetching the data.

Technologies:

Node NestJS Vue
